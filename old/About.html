About Myself

I'm Josh, I am an under graduate from Worcester University studying Computing. I have extensive knowledge of videography and photography through working with the family company - The Picture Workshop.

I have been working commercially since the summer of 2012 when I bought my first professional camera and have successfully completed a number of projects for different clients from all over the UK.

I have worked in the profession since I finished studying photography for A levels in 2010 and have been working as an assistant while studying at Worcester University, so I already have 3 years of assistant experience and 1 year of personal experience alongside the professional advice I can always obtain from the family company.

I only provide professional high quality media. I believe that if my client is not happy, the job is not complete.
