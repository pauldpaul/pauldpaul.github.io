About Me

Freelance photographer based in Worcester West Midlands, specialising in portraiture and still life photography. I recently finished my degree in Creative Digital Media specialising in photography at the University of Worcester achieving a 2.1. I have worked for Sabotage/ Fly 53, FT Images (weddings and portraiture photography), Moorlands studios and also assisting wedding photographer Andrea Dovey.
